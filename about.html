How it all started

The Mediterranean Project was founded by a fond traveller; Naomi, who has a deep passion for exploring diverse cultures, traditions, and world history. With a particular love for the Mediterranean region. After travelling around a large portion of the world, Naomi realised how much the region had a universal pull and how special it really was. It's culturally and historically rich region has some of the tastiest and healthiest cuisines in the world coupled with some of the most stunning landscapes and landmarks as old as time.
