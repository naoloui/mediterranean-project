About

How it started

The Mediterranean Project was founded by a fond traveller; Naomi, who has a deep passion for exploring diverse cultures, traditions, and world history. With a particular love for the Mediterranean region. After travelling around a large portion of the world, Naomi realised how much the region had a universal pull and how special it really was. It's culturally and historically rich region has some of the tastiest and healthiest cuisines in the world coupled with some of the most stunning landscapes and landmarks as old as time.

What kind of trips does the Mediterranean Project have to offer?

The Mediterranean Project offers a variety of trips that cater to different interests and preferences. Whether you're looking for a relaxing beach holiday, a cultural immersion experience, or an adventure-filled getaway. We have something for everyone. Our trips are designed to provide a balance of guided tours and free time, allowing you to explore at your own pace while also benefitting from the experience and knowledge of our local guides.

Target audience?

The Mediterranean Project is perfect for solo travellers, couples, families, and groups of friends who are looking for a unique and enriching travel experience. Whether you're a history buff, a food lover, or simply someone who enjoys exploring new cultures, our trips are designed to provide a memorable and fulfilling experience for all types of travellers. We welcome travellers of all ages and we provide package holidays that cater to different budgets; needs, age groups and interests.
